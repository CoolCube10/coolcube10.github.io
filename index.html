Cool Cube



What is Cool Cube?

Cool Cube is a student-led initiative that hopes to provide the STEM foundation that the next generation of engineers needs! Our three-pronged outreach approach features YouTube & Social Media, CubeSat kits, and 100% Free Tutoring!


YouTube & Social Media

As a way to make content as accessible as possible, we use YouTube to post videos about everything from problem solving skills to electronics tutorials.

CubeSat Kits

In a world that is constantly innovating, space exploration is becoming more and more accessible to students around the world. We hope to offer students the opportunity to apply their engineering knowledge by distributing a kit that creates a functioning satellite!

Free Tutoring

Tutoring gives us the opportunity to connect with students individually and help them become the best engineers that they can!

YouTube & Social Media

Share our experience

We use our social media to share updates, inspiration, cool facts, and much more. Our YouTube channel focusses on engineering and CubeSat-specific content!

CubeSat Kits

Help kids access tinker-able tech

We will be providing Title 1 schools across the DMV area with 3D printed model satellite kits that have real electronics. Our software starter-pack will help students jump into the world of satellite development and get a headstart on their STEM careers!

Free Tutoring

Offer high-quality STEM tutoring to the underpriveledged

We offer 100% free tutoring! All of our tutors are members of the TJREVERB satellite mission development team who would like to help kickstart the careers of the next generation of engineers. We tutor ANY student, regardless of background.
